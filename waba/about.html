Our Story

Masafi Water is a Water Refilling Station located in Thindigua, Kiambu County.
At Masafi, We specialize in purification and supply of water in various quantities ranging from half litre bottles to 20 litre bottles.
Purchases can be made in-store, through our online app or by calling our office number where we also offer FREE DELIVERY!!

Our Vision

We aim to be the go-to brand for all your water needs through effective service delivery and a customer-focused approach to business.

Our Mission

To provide the people of Thindigua and Kiambu at large with clean purified drinking water as well as create employment opportunities to the youth of the area.
